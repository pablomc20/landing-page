About me

Hi! My name is Pablo Cruz. I am a Web Developer Full Stack (Backe-End).

My experience covers a wide range of technologies. On the front-end, I have worked with CSS, HTML, JavaScript, and jQuery to create engaging and dynamic interfaces. Additionally, I have used frameworks like Bootstrap 5 to streamline development and ensure a smooth user experience.

On the backend, I have experience using languages such as PHP with the Laravel framework, Java with the Spring framework, and I have also dabbled in Python with the Django framework.

In terms of databases, I have worked with a variety of systems including MySQL, MariaDB, SQLite, and MongoDB. These projects have allowed me to develop skills in efficient database management and ensuring data integrity in high-performance environments.

Beyond my technical skills, I consider myself passionate about web development and API services. I enjoy tackling challenges and finding creative solutions to problems that arise in web system development.

I also value the importance of collaboration and knowledge sharing. I have had the opportunity to contribute to open-source projects, which has provided me with the experience of working in teams and learning from other talented developers.

In every project I undertake, my primary focus is to provide added value to my clients or users. I ensure I understand their needs and business objectives, and develop customized solutions that help them achieve success.

Whether it's optimizing the speed and performance of a system, ensuring the security of sensitive data, or enhancing the user experience, I always strive to exceed expectations and deliver exceptional results.

Throughout my career, I have achieved several notable milestones and overcome technical challenges. However, the most rewarding aspect for me is the satisfaction of my clients and end users.

What i do

Responsive Design

I create web designs that automatically adapt to different devices and screen sizes. I ensure an optimal user experience and intuitive navigation, allowing your website to look and perform excellently on mobile phones, tablets, and desktop computers.

Web Design

I create custom web systems that are tailored to your specific needs. From business management systems (ERP) to e-commerce platforms and more, I develop powerful and scalable web solutions that streamline your processes and improve the efficiency of your business.

Database

I design and develop efficient and secure databases for your project. From modeling to implementation, I ensure robust and optimized structures that guarantee data integrity and performance.

Clean code

I always apply clean coding standards and adhere to best practices to ensure well-structured, readable, and easily maintainable code. I use clear comments, proper naming conventions, and optimization techniques to ensure the quality and scalability of my projects.

API services

I provide custom API development services, creating robust and secure interfaces to facilitate communication between different systems. I design and develop RESTful API services using technologies like Spring Boot, FastAPI, and Django to ensure efficient performance and seamless integration with your applications and services.

Code version control

I provide code version control services using tools like Git, allowing you to have precise tracking of changes in your source code. I implement efficient branching and merging practices to ensure smooth collaborative development and a comprehensive history of revisions.
